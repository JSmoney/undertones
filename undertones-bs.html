Who are we?

Making music isn’t easy. Making unique music is even harder. Our team is full passionate producers who love to make underground music. About 2 years ago we were discussing the lack of sample packs, presets and tutorials for underground genres and since then we’ve been working in the shadows to continue mastering our craft and bring you the most inspiring sounds. In a short space of time we’ve built a great reputation for providing hard to find sounds and knowledge. Our goal is to inspire the next generation of artists and help breath new life into the music we love.

Jared Cave - Founder

Jared is a seasoned bedroom producer and mutli-instrumentalist who rose to prominence after a sold out gig at Luton Student Union as part of the genre blending, style transcending, 3 piece band, Reason. Since then the group have parted ways but the drummer/producer didn’t loose his taste for fusion and since then has continued to blend different flavors in search for a new special sauce.

Jonathan Cave - DJ

Jonathan is a true music connoisseur and embodiment of what we stand for here at Undertones. He has an electic and discriminating taste in music, picking out and showcasting the only best with no concern for conformity. After shutting down the legendary OCLA parties in Nottingham Jonathan has be mastering his craft and gearing up for a come back. Expect nothing from this man except vides. If it feels right, it is right. If sounds nice, he'll play it twice.

Adam James - UX Designer

Adam is a an ex sesh head turned born again christian. It's no wonder that his work has a devine touch. He has been working hard to understand how to deliver a platform that truly meets our customers needs and seperates us from the pack. After leaving the Army, Adam has been taking on and conquering mission after mission and creating the biggest music production community his next assignment. Over and out.
